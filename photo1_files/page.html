Kerala's Minister for Tourism, Mr. P. A Mohamed Riyas is in Madrid for FITUR - the Trade Fair for tourism professionals. He held a high-level meeting with UNWTO official Mr. Harry Hwang, Deputy Director, Regional Department for Asia and the Pacific, and his team, at the UNWTO headquarters. During the meeting the two teams held discussions on Kerala being the venue for hosting a major UNWTO global event and deliberated on possibilities of providing research support for sustainable practices audit and corrections in Kerala. The Minister also extended an invitation to the UNWTO team for the upcoming Responsible Tourism Global Summit.
